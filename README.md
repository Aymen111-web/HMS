# HMS (Hospital Management System)

This is a Hospital Management System (HMS) developed to streamline operations in hospitals, clinics, and other healthcare organizations. The HMS helps manage information about patients, doctors, appointments, treatments, billing, and more.

## Features

- **Patient Management:** Add, edit, and view patient records.
- **Doctor Management:** Manage information about doctors and their schedules.
- **Appointments:** Schedule, view, and manage appointments efficiently.
- **Treatment Records:** Track patient treatments, progress, and history.
- **Billing & Payments:** Generate invoices, process payments, and maintain histories.
- **Reports:** Generate and export administrative and medical reports.
- **User Authentication and Authorization:** Secure access for admins, staff, and doctors.
